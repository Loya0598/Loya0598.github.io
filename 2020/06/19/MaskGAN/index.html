MaskGAN

论文链接：https://arxiv.org/pdf/1801.07736.pdf

论文代码：https://github.com/tensorflow/models/tree/master/research/maskgan

阅读时间：2020.06.18

1、Simple Intro

  • 他们的任务模式是填空，不再是从头生成完整句子
  • 他们采用了actor-critic的框架去计算每一步的reward
  • 他们采用了一些新的evaluation metrics对他们的模型进行评估

2、Architecture

  • Generator

    • Model: $G(x_t) = P(\hat{x}_t | \hat{x}_1, …, \hat{x}_
      {t - 1}, m(x))$
    • 通过语言模型生成填空后的句子，比如“I want _ _ apple” 输出 “I want to pick apple”

  • Discriminator

    • Model: $D_{\phi}(\tilde{x_t}|\tilde{x}_{0:T}, m(x)) = P(\tilde{x}_t = x_t^{real} | \tilde{x}_{0:T}, m(x))$

    • 通过同样的语言模型，不再生成句子，而是取最后的分布概率中的正确token的概率，比如输入是”I want to pick apple”，真实数据是“I want this fresh apple”，经过语言模型我们会得到输出时每个token的一个分布概率，比如对于第一个mask的空位，得到的分布概率是{to: 0.6，this: 0.2，that: 0.2}，那么discriminator在这个token上的输出既是0.2。

    • Reward: $r_t = logD_{\phi}(\tilde{x}_t | \tilde{x}_{0:T}, m(x))$ 即为概率值取log

    • Reward at each position: $R_t = \sum_{s = t}^T \gamma^sr_s$

3、Training

  • Generator:

    • Reward at step t: $\mathbb{E}_G[R_t]$
      • 因为sample这个操作不可导（不可导的解释可以参照这一篇文章：Role of RL in text generation by GAN），所以我们可以利用RL中的policy gradient，将每一步的sample类比于RL中的采取动作，$\theta$即为我们的策略参数，我们期望能调整$\theta$来最大化reward。
    • Gradient of Reward at step t:
      • $\nabla_{\theta}\mathbb{E}_{G}[R_t] = \mathbb{E}_{G}[R_t\nabla_{\theta}logG_{\theta}(\hat{x}_t)]$
    • MiniBatch Update at each step(主要是想说明在实际用batch进行更新时loss要如何写):
      • Loss = $\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i = 1}^N(R_tlog(G_{\theta}(\hat{x}_t)))$
      • 因为我们的x可以看成是从G中采样的，所以对于上面那个gradient的期望，我们直接计算后半部分就可以了

  • Alternativa approaches for long sequences and large vocabularies

    • long sequences: 先在T这个长度将模型训练到收敛，再将其迁移到长度（T+1）上
    • Large vocabularies: 在生成每个token时，discriminator对每个可能的采样都输出reward，然后利用这个reward相应的去更新generator

4、Evaluation & Experiment

  • Datasets:

    • The Penn Treebank(PTB)

      We first pretrain the commonly-used variational LSTM language model with parameter dimensions common to MaskGAN following Gal & Ghahramani (2016) to a validation perplexity of 78. After then loading the weights from the language model into the MaskGAN generator we further pretrain with a masking rate of 0.5 (half the text blanked) to a validation perplexity of 55.3. Finally, we then pretrain the discriminator on the samples produced from the current generator and real training text.

    • IMDB Movie Dataset

      Identical to the training process in PTB, we pretrain a language model to a validation perplexity of 105.6. After then loading the weights from the language model into the MaskGAN generator we further pretrain with masking rate of 0.5 (half the text blanked) to a validation perplexity of 87.1. Finally, we then pretrain the discriminator on the samples produced from the current generator and real training text.
